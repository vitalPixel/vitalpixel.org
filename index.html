Hey, I'm Pico!

This is meant to be a very simple on-line presence, kind of a mix between the quirky and wonderful personal homepages from the 90s and the low-tech blogs of the early 00s.

The one goal of this website is to create transparency; to act like a window, or a storefront, that proudly displays whatever organic evolution is happening inside. And hopefully it will drag me – and you – along in that same evolutionary process!

If you'd like to reach out, DM me on twitter or send me an e-mail.

I'm currently interested in Regeneration

I love to design (or to designate, or to specify) all kinds of things by writing and having discussions, drawing or making diagrams, or by traveling and taking photos... I particularly like to do all of this while dancing and listening to music.

Currently, I'm extremely interested in regeneration: earth regeneration, bioregional regeneration, land and soil regeneration, community regeneration, architectural regeneration, individual regeneration...

I'm particularly interested in how regeneration relates to an urban context. To that extent, my girlfriend and I are currently building our home in my hometown (Porto). We call it "A Bataria 127" and we plan to build it in public as much as possible. For now, it's just a small website (in Portuguese) that we're mainly using to communicate with the architects.
