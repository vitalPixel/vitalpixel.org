Hi, I'm Pico and I'm a designer.

This website (and myself along with it) is being "designed in the open"; you should check out Frank Chimero's project (where I kind of stole the idea from) because it's infinetely more useful than this one.

In the meantime, you can find me on twitter and on on medium, or take a look at my LinkedIn profile. I ocasionally post stuff on instagram too.

Who am I?

I like too think that I was born a designer. Professionally, I feel like a product manager that has been wearing multiple product hats for almost 10 years and is currently working as a product owner. I'm constantly looking to improve the way by which content is delivered to humans (especially in digital spaces).

I love to design (or to designate, or to specify) by writing, having discussions, drawing, taking photos, or making diagrams... I particularly like to do it while traveling and/or listening to music.

Currently, I'm extremely interested in borders (and crossing them), in politics (or political science) and in complexity theories.

What is this?

This website is an attempt to force me to do 3 things:

  1. Better organize my thoughts arround issues that are critical to me, by writing more;
  2. Improve those thoughts by exposing (part of) them to a public audience;
  3. Keep up to date with modern HTML and CSS (and maybe some very light JavaScript).

The next post will hopefully explain the project a little further.
